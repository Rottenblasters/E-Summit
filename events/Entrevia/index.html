Entrevia

About

An entrepreneurship quiz

Test your knowledge and analytical skills to deep-dive into the world of entrepreneurship and business. Questions would be from all walks of the general entrepreneurship/startup ecosystem as well as business buzz. Read the Questions carefully and you might as well be able to crack the answers from the hints in the question itself!

Round Details

Preliminary Round

In the preliminary round, the Quiz Master will be presenting 20 questions in the meet and a google form will be provided for submitting the answers. There will be star-marked questions to break ties.

Final Round

The __(based on participants) top-scorers of this round will qualify for the finals, which also will be held on the same day.

Rules and Description:

  • The event will be having two rounds: Preliminary and Finals
  • The preliminary round is to be hosted on a meet from ___ on _____ 2022
  • In the preliminary round, the quiz master will be presenting 20 questions in the meet and a Google Form will be provided for submitting the answers
  • There will be star-marked questions to break ties
  • If there is still a tie, sudden death from the first question to the last will be used to break the tie
  • In order to ensure fairness, participants will be asked to switch on their cameras while attempting the quiz
  • We strongly recommend the participants use vocal methods, (like Discord Voice Channel, Phone Call, etc )to communicate with their teammates (if any)
  • The 8 top-scorers of this round will qualify for the finals, which also will be held on the same day
  • Teams of 1 or 2 members are allowed
